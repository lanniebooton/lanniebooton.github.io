About

Hailing from the UK and living in NZ, I am an award-winning multidisciplinary artist with two decades of experience across illustration, game development, teaching, television, short film, and sculpture.
Though I'm a versatile creator and work in a wide range of styles, I have a love for horror art, fantasy, and sci-fi. Some of my biggest inspirations being Jim Henson, Tim Burton, H.R Giger, Jim Moir, and Yoshitaka Amano.
I love to experiment and play with new mediums, and like many artists probably have a few too many hobbies. I'm also a programmer and have an interest in Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and a few programming languages that I use to make largely ridiculous games or tools.
One day I'd love to get into tattooing and I suspect that'd be the next realm I explore when able! As it stands I currently work in the animation industry as a technical artist for television productions. Previously I have also worked in compositing/VFX, editing, storyboarding, and screenwriting.

Traditional

I exhibit around North Canterbury with mixed media paintings and sculpture work. I am available for commissions and bespoke items. Please contact me if you'd like to work together on a project.

Contact

I am available for commissions in the following scope:

  • Artwork (digital/traditional, international shipping available)
  • Sculpture (NZ/Aus only)
  • Screenwriting, editing, post production services
  • Game prototyping
  • Narrative and design consulting for screen and games

Myself and my partner Dane Jacobs also collaborate and offer full animation pipelines for projects, from concept to delivery.
Please note that it's really appreciated if you have a budget and deadline in mind and can include this information in your email.
